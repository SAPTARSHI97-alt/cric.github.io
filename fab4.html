THE "FAB4"

The Fab Four - short for 'Fabulous Four' - is a term coined by cricket pundits which clubs together the top four Test batters of the modern era - India's Virat Kohli, Joe Root, Steve Smith and New Zealand's Kane Williamson.



Root averaged 97.1 in 11 innings, Smith averaged 81.9 in 17 innings, Kane Williamson averaged 61.9 in 17 innings, and Kohli averaged 44.6 in 20 innings


FAB4 in Test- JOE ROOT is the dominator, no doubt. Virat lacks behind at no.2!


FAB4 in ODI- VIRAT KOHLI is the king having best average and 12000+ runs.


FAB4 in T20I- Again VIRAT KOHLI shines! He has 3000+ runs which is much more than the rest FAB4.
